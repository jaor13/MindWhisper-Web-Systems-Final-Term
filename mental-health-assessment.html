PTSD Assessment

1. Have you experienced a traumatic event in your life?

2. How often do you experience intrusive memories or flashbacks of the event?

3. Do certain triggers or reminders cause intense emotional distress or physical reactions?

4. How frequently do you avoid situations, places, or people that remind you of the traumatic event?

5. Are you experiencing changes in mood or cognition, such as negative beliefs, guilt, or detachment?





Anxiety Assessment

1. How often do you feel restless or on edge?

2. Do you struggle to control worrying thoughts?

3. How frequently do you experience muscle tension or headaches?

4. Are you easily fatigued or irritable?

5. How often do you struggle with concentration due to anxious thoughts?

Eating Disorder Assessment

1. Do you frequently worry about your weight or body image?

2. Do you engage in episodes of binge eating or consuming large amounts of food in a short period?

3. Do you feel a loss of control during episodes of eating?

4. Do you often engage in unhealthy behaviors to prevent weight gain, such as fasting or excessive exercise?

5. Do you experience feelings of guilt, shame, or distress after eating?
