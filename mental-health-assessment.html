Mental Health Test

Please select the test you would like to take:

PTSD Test

Please answer the following questions based on how often you have experienced each symptom in the past month:

1. Intrusive thoughts about a traumatic event.
2. Avoiding activities that remind you of a traumatic event.
3. Flashbacks or nightmares about a traumatic event.
4. Hyperarousal or feeling on edge.
5. Hypervigilance or heightened sensitivity to surroundings.
6. Difficulty sleeping or staying asleep.
7. Irritability or anger outbursts.
8. Feelings of detachment or emotional numbness.
9. Difficulty concentrating or focusing.
10. Avoidance of people, places, or activities.

Depression Test

Please answer the following questions based on how often you have experienced each symptom in the past month:

1. Persistent sadness or low mood.
2. Loss of interest or pleasure in activities.
3. Changes in appetite or weight.
4. Fatigue or loss of energy.
5. Feelings of worthlessness or excessive guilt.
6. Muscle tension or physical symptoms of anxiety.
7. Difficulty controlling worries or intrusive thoughts.
8. Feelings of restlessness or being on edge.
9. Fear of losing control or going crazy.
10. Avoidance of situations that trigger anxiety.

Anxiety Test

Please answer the following questions based on how often you have experienced each symptom in the past month:

1. Excessive worry or apprehension.
2. Restlessness or feeling on edge.
3. Difficulty concentrating.
4. Difficulty falling or staying asleep.
5. Irritability or feeling easily annoyed.
6. Feelings of hopelessness or pessimism.
7. Difficulty concentrating or making decisions.
8. Thoughts of death or suicide.
9. Changes in sleep patterns (insomnia or oversleeping).
10. Loss of interest in personal hygiene or appearance.

Eating Disorder Test

Please answer the following questions based on how often you have experienced each symptom in the past month:

1. Preoccupation with food, weight, or body shape.
2. Engaging in binge eating episodes.
3. Engaging in compensatory behaviors such as vomiting or excessive exercise.
4. Distorted body image or dissatisfaction with body weight or shape.
5. Feeling out of control during episodes of eating.
6. Feeling guilty or ashamed about eating habits.
7. Feeling out of control while eating.
8. Obsessive thoughts about food or body image.
9. Isolation or avoidance of social situations involving food.
10. Using food as a means to cope with emotions.
